Tochitura

What a great tasting dish!

Also known as Pork Stew, this dish looks nothing like a real stew, but the taste, texture and smell are mind-twisting!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Pork Sausages and Ribs
  • Bacon
  • Pork Tenderloin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Bay Leaves

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the pot over medium-high, add vegetable oil, sliced bacon and cook for 7-8 minutes to render the fat.
  2. Add sliced pork tenderloin and sliced sausages, stir well to coat the tenderloin with fat. Add some paprika and cook for about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and saute for 1-2 minutes then pour in water along with a couple of bay leaves, cover and cook on medium to low heat for about 30 minutes
  4. Final step would be to add all the herbs and cook it for another 10 minutes, covered.
